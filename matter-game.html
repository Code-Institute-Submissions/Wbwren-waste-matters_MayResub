How to play

Goal: fill the compactor with as many polystyrene blocks as you can.

  • Click and hold the button to spawn blocks.

  • Click and drag the blocks to stack as high as you can.

  • You have 3 lives, a life will be taken if a block falls outside of the compactor.

  • When you think you have filled the compactor as much as you can, click the button to save your score!

  • Click the button to play again.
